Saab summer intern

I was a summer intern at Saab in Linköping 2011, 2012, 2013 and 2014. The first three years I converted analog documents to digital. 2014 I developed a system in the flight test department for the control room where clients communicate with a server where the flight leader sees the status for every client whether they are ready to go or not. The system was written in Java and I both tested and developed both the client side and server side of the system.
